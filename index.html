A Tale of ℤ12 cyclic group.

We will start with the ordinary way of telling time from clocks like the one shown below.

The clock shows it is 4:00 at the moment. We know that since the hour hand points to a symbol called number 4 and the minute hand points to another symbol called number 12.

In essence, it seems we require such symbols (we call numbers) to read time from the clock. But are those symbols (numbers) necessary, just to read time?

We will later see that is not so, as we gradually build a time teller that does not use numbers, instead use simple polygonal shapes. In our journey, we will also take a peek on Group Theory, in particular the cyclic group of ℤ12.
